Why does Edinburg hard water affect my plumbing?

Like the rest of the RGV, Edinburg has hard water that builds scale in pipes, water heaters, and fixtures over time. It shortens water heater life and reduces flow. We can help with descaling, fixture upgrades, and water heater replacement.
